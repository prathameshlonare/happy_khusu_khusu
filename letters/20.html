Dear Future Piku,
To piku jo aaj tuzya khusu sobat ahe, ani tuzha khusu sobatch swapna sakar zala ahe.

Mi, Present Piku, aaj he letter lihat ahe, jo khusu pasun physically dur ahe, pn manane ti mazya nemhi sobat aste. amhi doghe pn amchya sundar future chi, sobat asnya chi, apla ghar asnya chi swapna baghat asto. mala mahit ahe tu future piku to te swapna jagat ahes.

Mala tula kahi goshti athvan karun dyaychya aahet, je kadhich visru nakos:

  • Saglyat adhi, khusu la kadhich for granted dharu nakos . Tine aaplya sathi, ya relation sathi kiti sacrifices kele aahet, kiti patience thevla aahe, he nehmich lakshat aasu de. Tichya prema chi kimmat kar.
  • Relation la nehmich fresh aani exciting thevnyasathi prayatna karat raha. Chotya chotya surprises de, tila special feel karav. Tichya chehrya vr smile he tuzhya sathi saglyat motha gift asude.
  • Kadhihi communication gap yeu deu naka. Kitihi busy asal, tari ekmekanshi manmokla bolat raha, feelings share karat raha.
  • Aaple te long distance che divas athav. Tevha kashi ekmekana saath dili,kashi chotya chotya goshtinmadhun anand shodhat hota. Tyach pramane, pudhehi pratek moment japun theva.
  • Khushuchi nehmich kalaji ghe. Tila support kar. Tila hasavat raha.

Mala ek prashna aahe tuzyasathi (Future Piku): Khushu aaj pan tashich god haste ka, jasa mala aaj athavtay? mala mahit ahe tuzya sobat ti ankhi khush asel.

Mi (Present Piku) tuza (Future Piku) aani Khushucha khup abhari aahe ki tumhi doghe sobat itke god life jagat ahat. mazya kadun khusu la khup motha ani khup premane hug deshil.

Tumchya (Aaplya) sundar ayushyasathi khup khup shubhechha! Enjoy every moment.

Aaplya Bhavishyachi Swapna Baghnara,
Present Piku
