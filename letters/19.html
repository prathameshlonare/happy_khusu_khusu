Mazhi Dear Khushu,

baghta baghta aplya la 4 years houn gele khusu, mi tula sangu nahi shekat tuzya sobat spend kele le he 4 yrs kashe nighun gele mahiti suddha nahi padle, ani aplya madhala bonding pn itka strong ani chan houn gela ki khup chan vatte ha vichar karun.

ya sagdya badalan madhe, ya long distance madhe ya sarv kahi problems madhe, ek gosht ji aplya madhe kahich nahi baddalli, Aani ti goshta mhanje – "Apla eka mekavr cha trust, apla prem, ani apli sath".

khusu kitihi apn dur aslo, aplya madhe kiti pn physical antar asla, ani aplya madhe kiti pn problems zalet tari pn apla eka meka vrcha trust nahi tutla, apla prem kami nahi zala, ulat time chya sobat vadatach gela ahe.

Aapn ekmekana samjhaun ghetla, ekmekana support kela, aani saglyat mahatvacha mhanje ekmekanvar trust thevla. ani hich ti gosht ahe ji aplya la dur rahun suddha javad karun thevte ani bandhun thevte.

mala purn bharosa ahe ki kiti pn varsha jaude, aplya madhe kiti pn problems yeu de, tari pn apn asach rahnar ahot, ani ashech eka mekachi sath denar ahot. ❤️
